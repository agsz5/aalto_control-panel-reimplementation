  • Co-creating new business opportunities in unexplored emerging markets through multi-stakeholder partnership networks

The New Global is an innovation project integrating knowledge and technology from multiple sectors and stakeholders into complete, sustainable commercial solutions. It combines academic research and private sector’s business development in order to jointly innovate in developing high business potential markets. The project is led by Aalto University (Helsinki, Finland), and mostly funded by Tekes (The Finnish Funding Agency for Innovation). The project was launched in January 2014, and its first phase is two years long.

Through learning-by-doing, the New Global incorporates a pragmatic approach to creating the necessary capabilities for co-creating frugal and reverse innovations in complex global systems. The New Global will provide on-the-ground experimentation, through well-established partnerships and networks, in a growing number of emerging markets including India, Tanzania, Mexico and Sri Lank

We will create multi-partner, multi-country collaboration bridging Finnish companies and local entrepreneurs. Our multidisciplinary action research provides necessary facilitation for action, as well as summons the collective learning. The approach can be summarized as “Think big and global, act local, start small, utilize the network”.

Through the New Global, companies will learn the logic of developing frugal and reverse innovations together with other partners and local communities. They will gain the opportunity to innovate business models around top technologies, and embed solutions into local ecosystems in emerging growth markets, and eventually grow with the growth markets.

Key capabilities developed in the process include understanding and being able to conduct the co-creation process from fuzzy, complex problems to simple, affordable, and functioning solutions. This creates pathways for the outstanding solutions developed by Finnish companies to global growth markets. The co-creation approach enables to discover the local needs, opportunities, and lifestyles, and therefore to develop innovations based on them. Involving local stakeholders in the co-creation process also creates ownership for the solution developed, and therefore creates market demand, already during the co-creation process.
